She built a tree-house in the tree to live in and stop the company from cutting down the forest. Also she told her family and news reporters about this event. With the help of environmental organizations and other people, she was successful. The company agreed to stop cutting the forest. Finally, Julia Hill climbed down and walked on the ground again.
